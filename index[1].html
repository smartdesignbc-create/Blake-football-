How To Play

  1. Swipe the ball — drag from the ball and release toward the goal. Direction and speed of your swipe set the shot's power and curve.
  2. Hit the targets — moving gold rings appear inside the goal. Strike them to score.
  3. Centre targets are worth 100, corner targets 250, and the rare moving bonus target is worth 500.
  4. Chain shots quickly to build a streak bonus, and keep your accuracy high for an accuracy bonus at the end.
  5. You have 60 seconds. Score as much as you can before the timer runs out.
